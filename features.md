# Cricket Architect

Build your cricket empire from the ground up.

Cricket Architect is a global franchise management game where you choose one city, grow unknown talent, outsmart rival clubs, and climb a four-league pyramid to become world champion.

## Why Players Will Love It
- Start with a raw, low-rated squad and build a dynasty.
- Manage a real long-term career, not one-off matches.
- Compete in a full 52-club global ecosystem.
- Feel every decision through growth, form, finance, and results.
- Live the drama with match center, scorecards, and ball-by-ball action.

## Core Experience
- Pick your city and create your club identity.
- Develop players through a youth academy and regional pipeline.
- Play home-and-away league seasons with promotion and relegation stakes.
- Win your league to reach semifinals and fight for the final trophy.
- Track your club’s rise through trophies, valuation growth, and historical records.

## Signature Features

### 1) Global Franchise Career
- Choose your city from a worldwide catalog.
- Own one club and shape its future season after season.
- Build fan momentum and club value through performance.

### 2) Four-League World Pyramid
- 52 clubs across Leagues 1 to 4.
- Every league runs a full home-and-away season.
- Top team in each league reaches semifinals.
- Bottom teams go down, top teams go up every year.

### 3) Youth Academy and Talent Development
- Run a realistic academy with region-based prospect generation.
- Spend points to upgrade academy quality and player growth systems.
- Promote youth prospects into your first-team setup.

### 4) Match Day Drama
- Full T20 simulation with toss, innings flow, and pressure situations.
- Dedicated Match Center with innings tabs and clear score presentation.
- Ball-by-ball commentary, over summaries, and performance breakdowns.
- Batting and bowling scorecards for both innings.

### 5) Living Club Economy
- Club valuation grows from wins, streaks, trophies, pipeline quality, and squad strength.
- Active transfer ecosystem including auction, loans, and CPU club activity.
- Financial and valuation tracking to measure long-term growth.

### 6) Player Stories and History
- Persistent player and team records across seasons.
- Career progression from unknown prospects to match winners.
- Trophy room and seasonal archives to showcase your legacy.

## Designed for Long-Term Play
Cricket Architect is built for players who love management depth, career progression, and the feeling of turning a bottom club into a world-class cricket institution.

## Positioning
Cricket Architect is the modern cricket management career experience: global, competitive, and built around the joy of building something great over time.
